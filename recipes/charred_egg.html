Teyvat Charred Egg

Description

I bring to you Bennett's signature dish, the amazing Teyvat Charred Egg! This is an amazing fried egg that is said to bring you great misfortune as well as a good source of protein. The egg yolk will be nice and runny while the bottom with have a nice charred crunchy texture. After eating this you will definitely give a thumbs up!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 green onion

    • Steps

    1. Chop your green onions, place them in a bowl and set it aside.
    2. Add some oil, or butter to a pan and warm it up.
    3. When the pan is warmed up crack your egg into the pan and set the temperature to high.
    4. Cook until your egg whites are no longer translucent and the plate.
    5. Garnish the egg with your chopped green onions and sprinkle a bit of salt and pepper then enjoy!
